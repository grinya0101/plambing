Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Roof Quote USA collects and uses information submitted through this website.

Information we collect

We may collect your name, phone number, email address, ZIP code, homeowner status, requested service, project timeline, and details you choose to provide about your roofing request.

How information is used

Information may be used to process your roofing estimate request, contact you about your request, route your inquiry to a roofing service provider, improve the website, and comply with legal obligations.

Sharing of information

Your request details may be shared with service providers, contractors, technology vendors, or business partners involved in responding to your inquiry. We do not sell information for unrelated advertising purposes.

Consent to contact

By submitting a form, you agree to be contacted about your request by phone, text, or email. Consent is not a condition of purchase. Message and data rates may apply.

Security

No website can guarantee complete security. Use accurate information only when you are comfortable being contacted about your request.

Your choices

You may request that we stop contacting you by replying to any communication or using the contact details provided by the site owner.

Important note

This is a template. Before using this website commercially, review this policy with a qualified professional and update it to match your exact business model, vendors, and data practices.
